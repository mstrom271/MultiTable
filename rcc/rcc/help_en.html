Multiplication Table

 

Master multiplication with our application. It will collect statistics of your answers and identify your strengths and weaknesses!


Statistics are represented by the table, where different tints of green and red colors show how good or bad each pair of numbers are studied. Intelligent algorithm will select poorly learned combinations and offer them more often.


Try to answer correctly, but if you forget, don't hesitate to use prompts - it will help our algorithm. The application takes notice of answer speed and used prompts.


Also the table can be resized at your discretion. Our advice is to start with small table and add one line of numbers when the speed of your answers will increase to several seconds.
